About

My name is Garance Pröbsting, I’m a French-German product designer based in Berlin specializing in Object design for hospitality and residential contexts. Rooted in an education at the School of Applied Arts in Paris and at the University of the Arts in Berlin, where I am currently pursuing my master’s degree, I dedicate my practice to creating objects that become companions in our lives, mediators of our daily rituals and active presences shaping the way we inhabit and experience our environments.
